# What is Dualism?

Defines [dualism](../Definitions/dualism.md) and briefly touches on it's difference from [monism](../Definitions/monism.md) and [pluralism](../Definitions/pluralism.md).

- Monism says that reality is one.

- Pluralism says that reality is made up of many different kinds of substances.

[Plato](../People/Plato.md) was the first dualist with his [Theory of Forms](../Zettels/Theory%20of%20Forms.md), and 17th and 18th century philosophers such as [Rene Descartes](../People/Rene%20Descartes.md) resurrected it.
