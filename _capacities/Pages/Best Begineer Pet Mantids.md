# Best Begineer Pet Mantids

Top beginner [Mantids](../Zettels/Mantids.md) include:

1. [Chinese Mantis (Tenodera sinensis)](../Species/Chinese%20Mantis%20(Tenodera%20sinensis).md) -> big, easy to find, natural temperatures, normal humidity, and not a picky eater. One downside, it's not very pretty, just brown and no camouflage.

2. [Giant Asian Mantis (Hierodula membranacea)](../Zettels/Giant%20Asian%20Mantis%20(Hierodula%20membranacea).md) -> just like the Chinese mantis, but it's more beautiful, not as easy to find, and a bit more prone to food infections.

3. [Budwig Mantis (Parasphendale argrionina)](../Species/Budwig%20Mantis%20(Parasphendale%20argrionina).md) -> a bit harder than the previous two, and females are ferocious predators with their own threatening pose to fend off predators.

4. [African Mantis (Sphodromantis lineola)](../Species/African%20Mantis%20(Sphodromantis%20lineola).md) -> easy care, normal humidity, average temperatures, eats almost anything, but also a bit boring and hard to find because they're only bred as pets.

5. [Ghost Mantis (Phyllocrania paradoxa)](../Species/Ghost%20Mantis%20(Phyllocrania%20paradoxa).md) -> despite being so popular, it's actually the least suitable for beginners because it needs high humidity, slightly higher-than-average temperatures, and eats mostly flies and moths. However, unlike it's cousins, this species looks amazing and can actually be a communal option.
