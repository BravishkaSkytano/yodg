# How big should a crochet bag be?

The size of your crochet bag depends on what you’ll use it for. Here’s a quick guide:

- **Everyday handbag**: 10–12 inches wide, 8–10 inches tall

- **Tote bag**: 14–16 inches wide, 12–14 inches tall

- **Mini bag**: 6–8 inches wide, 5–7 inches tall

- **Beach bag**: 16–18 inches wide, 14–16 inches tall

- **Crossbody or sling**: 8–10 inches wide, 10–12 inches tall



A flap adds a nice look and helps keep things secure.



If you just want to fit a book, phone, and iPad, a **medium messenger bag** will work great:

- **Width**: 11–12 inches

- **Height**: 9–10 inches

- **Depth**: 2–3 inches

- **Flap**: About 8 inches long with a rounded edge

- **Strap**: Around 45 inches for crossbody comfort
