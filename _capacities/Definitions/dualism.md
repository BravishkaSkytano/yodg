# dualism

Composed of two different fundamental substances.

- The world consists of two fundamental substances, a material one and an immaterial one.

- In [Metaphysics](./Metaphysics.md), these two substances would be physical bodies and spiritual minds that exist independent of each other.

- Considered the middle ground between [monism](./monism.md) and [pluralism.](./pluralism.md)

- Earliest known dualist was [Plato](../People/Plato.md) with his [Theory of Forms](../Zettels/Theory%20of%20Forms.md), and it was resurrected in the 17th and 18th centuries by dualist thinkers like [Rene Descartes](../People/Rene%20Descartes.md).
