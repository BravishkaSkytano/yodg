# Nominalism

Universals don't exist, they're just ideas used to label particulars.

Similar to [Realism](./Realism.md), but [universals](./universals.md) become ideas that we create in our minds to categorize, describe, and understand [particulars](./particulars.md). Universals have no real existence in the material world.
