# moderate realism

A belief within [realism](./Realism.md); particulars are all that truly exists in the material world, and universals are the indistinct qualities that classify them.

A belief within [Realism](./Realism.md) that states that [particulars](./particulars.md) are all that exist, while [universals](./universals.md) are only qualities that are not distinct from the particular. It has a lot of similarities to [Nominalism](./Nominalism.md), only it argues for the existence of universals despite their indistinctiveness from particulars.
