# platonic realism

An idea within [realism](./Realism.md), states that all particulars are perfect representations of their Form from the World of Forms.

An idea within [Realism](./Realism.md) based on [Plato](../People/Plato.md)'s belief in the [World of Forms](../Zettels/World%20of%20Forms.md). It states that all [particulars](./particulars.md) are perfect representations of their Form from the World of Forms.
