# trope nominalism

All properties exist with the particulars as bundles of tropes.

A belief within [Nominalism](./Nominalism.md) that was formed to combat the [Problem of Companionship](../Zettels/Problem%20of%20Companionship.md). All properties exist as part of the [particulars](./particulars.md) that classify and are renamed as tropes. Each particular has a bundle of these tropes, with some tropes resembling tropes within the trope bundles of other particulars.
