# Budwig Mantis (Parasphendale argrionina)

***Parasphendale agrionina*** species of [praying mantis](https://en.wikipedia.org/api/rest_v1/page/mobile-html/Praying_mantis) in the family [Miomantidae](https://en.wikipedia.org/api/rest_v1/page/mobile-html/Miomantidae). It has been given the [common name](https://en.wikipedia.org/api/rest_v1/page/mobile-html/Common_name) **budwing mantis** for its [vestigial](https://en.wikipedia.org/api/rest_v1/page/mobile-html/Vestigial) [wings](https://en.wikipedia.org/api/rest_v1/page/mobile-html/Wing)(present in adult females). Females are incapable of [flight](https://en.wikipedia.org/api/rest_v1/page/mobile-html/Flight).[[1]](https://en.wikipedia.org/api/rest_v1/page/mobile-html/Parasphendale_agrionina#cite_note-1)
