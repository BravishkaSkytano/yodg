# March 28, 2025

In [Book of Numbers](../Pages/Book%20of%20Numbers.md) 25, why did God have the name of the Middianite woman remembered? An Israelite brought a Middianite woman into the camp to have sexual relations with her, despite the plague that God was using to punish such behavior. One of the sons of the priests killed both of them with a spear, acting zealously. Afterwards, the Bible mentions the name of all three of them, why was she included? #biblestudy
