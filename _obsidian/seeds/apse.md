> [!summary]
> A semicircular or polygonal recess at the end of a church, usually behind the altar. It often has a domed or [[Vault]]ed ceiling and serves as a focal point for worship, sometimes decorated with [[20251012200052|Mosaic]]s or frescoes.

**Example:** The apse [[20251012200052|Mosaic]] in the Church of San Vitale in Ravenna, Italy.
