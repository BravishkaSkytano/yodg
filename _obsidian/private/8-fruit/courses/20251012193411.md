> [!summary]
> A type of **church or shrine built over the grave of a martyr** or a site connected to a holy event. Martyrium buildings often have a **centralized plan** (like a circle or octagon) to focus attention on the sacred spot.
>
> **Example:** [[The Church of the Holy Sepulchre]] in **Jerusalem**, built over the site believed to be Jesus’s tomb.
