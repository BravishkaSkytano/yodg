> [!summary]
> Refers to the art and architecture of the [[🌰 Byzantine Empire|Byzantine Empire]] (about 330–1453 CE). It’s known for its **religious themes**, **gold backgrounds**, **flat, stylized figures**, and **mosaics** that emphasize **spirituality over [[realism|Realism]]**.

Byzantine artwork is still maintained in Eastern Orthodox countries, specifically Greece, Cyprus, Serbia, Bulgaria, Romania, and Russia.
