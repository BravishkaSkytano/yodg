> [!tldr] Flying Buttress
> An **external support structure** that transfers the weight of a building’s tall walls and roof away from the main structure to a separate pier, allowing for **thinner walls and large stained glass windows**. It’s a hallmark of **[[20251012184431|Gothic]] architecture**, giving buildings both strength and elegance.
> 
> ![[Flying Buttress-1760315669597.webp|Flying Buttress of Notre Dame Cathedral|250x143]]

^0b390b

**Example:** The **flying buttresses of [[Notre-Dame de Paris]]** in **Paris**.
